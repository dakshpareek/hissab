## Publishing rules

1. Only public, source-backed information gets published.
2. Every timeline entry should have a URL or a named public reference.
3. No original claims of guilt, blame, or motive.
4. If something is uncertain, the page should say so.
5. Corrections should be visible, not hidden.

## Status language

The site should describe the legal or administrative stage of a case, not editorial conclusions.
