The Ghatkopar hoarding was reportedly far larger than what its BMC permit allowed. Multiple civic accountability questions remain unanswered: how did an undersized permit translate into a 250-tonne structure? Who inspected it? The BMC officer's suspension — not arrest — reflects the standard outcome for government officials in such cases.
