# Source Policy

The case finder is a discovery layer. It should favor sources that are most likely to surface real, material incidents without overclaiming.

## Source priority
1. Official agency, court, regulator, or company releases
2. Named newsroom articles with direct article URLs
3. Wire reports published on newsroom pages
4. RSS / feed excerpts and topic pages used as discovery leads
5. Official social posts from verified agency or company accounts

## What to look for
- brand new incident reports
- meaningful follow-up updates
- legal or judicial milestones
- compensation / ex-gratia updates
- identity clarifications that make a case easier to track

## What to avoid
- generic homepage links
- pure syndication duplicates
- comment-only or snippet-only pages that never identify the incident clearly
- social posts from unofficial accounts

## Rule of thumb
If the source cannot clearly support a queueable discovery signal, drop it or hold it for review rather than forcing a case.
