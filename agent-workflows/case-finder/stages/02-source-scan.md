# Source Scan

## Objective
Search trusted public sources for new incident leads and material updates tied to the registry.

## What to scan
- official notices and releases
- named publication articles
- wire reports
- RSS/feed items and topic pages used as discovery leads
- official social posts from agencies or companies when they contain a credible incident lead

## Output
- candidate article URLs
- publication/source names
- headline snippets
- article dates when available

## Rules
- Prefer direct article URLs.
- Limit scan depth so the workflow stays focused.
- Use the registry aliases to expand queries.
- Do not freeze source text.
- Do not write queue items yet.
