Title: 2 Dead After BMW Z4 Crashes Into Divider At 250 Kmph, Flips On Highway
Meta description: Mumbai BMW Z4 crash on Mumbai-Vadodara Highway kills two, injures one, police probe high speed, possible alcohol use and forensic findings underway.
Published time: 2026-06-21T21:28:05+05:30

Extracted body text:
Two people were killed and one sustained serious injuries as a BMW car rammed into a divider and then overturned multiple times on the Mumbai-Vadodara Highway in Mumbai’s Badlapur in the early hours of Sunday.
The incident took place at around 4 am on a stretch of the Mumbai-Vadodara highway near Badlapur when the victims were returning from Titwala.
According to preliminary investigation, the BMW Z4, allegedly being driven at 250kmph, hit the road divider and overturned multiple times before coming to a halt. The impact of the crash left the car in a mangled state.
PTI quoted a police official saying that the deceased, identified as Yogesh Dighe (26) and Rikeba Jacob (24), were residents of Badlapur and the person who suffered serious injuries was identified as Anand. He is undergoing treatment at a private hospital in Thane, he said.
NDTV quoted witnesses saying that the accident was so severe that body parts were found scattered several metres away.
“A case of accidental death has been registered, and further investigation is underway," the station house officer of Badlapur police station said.
Police further said that a forensic team has collected evidence from the accident site and an investigation is ongoing into all aspects of the case, including whether the driver was under the influence of alcohol.
